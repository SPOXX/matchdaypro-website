Account deletion

Just like when your favorite player leaves your team, we're sad to see you go. But we respect your decision, and here's how you can do it:

1. Sending us an email

a. Send an email to support@matchday.pro with the subject "Account Deletion Request."
b. In the email, tell us your username and the email you use for the account.
c. Our support team will review your request, and even though we're sad to see you leave, we'll guide you on how to delete your account.

2. Within the app

a. Open the Matchday Pro app.
b. Click on the hamburger icon and look for "Delete your account" in the menu.
c. Follow the on-screen steps. They're easy, just like a pass to your favorite player.
d. Once you confirm, your account will be set for deletion. Be aware, it's permanent, your data will be gone.

Please note that it can take up to 72 hours before your account is fully deleted.
During this time, your data will be gradually removed from our systems.

Remember, we'll miss having you around, like a team misses a star player.
Deleting your account is a big move.
Make sure to save anything important before you go.

If you have questions or need help, just reach out to our support team at support@matchday.pro.
We're here to assist you with any concerns you may have, and we'll always welcome you back with open arms if you decide to return.
