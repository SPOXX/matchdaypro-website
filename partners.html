Partnership with The AXXS Foundation

At Matchday Pro, we understand the transformative power of sports. We believe in the values of equality, inclusivity, and the right of everyone to access and participate in sports, regardless of their background or circumstances. This belief has led us to form a meaningful partnership with The AXXS Foundation.

The AXXS Foundation is a renowned organisation with a mission to democratise access to sports, breaking down barriers and fostering an environment free from discrimination. The Foundation recognises the invaluable life lessons sports can teach - cooperation, perseverance, and the ability to cope with both success and defeat - lessons that extend beyond the pitch and improve the overall quality of life.

The AXXS Foundation is delighted to partner with Matchday Pro, a platform that shares our belief in the power of sports. Together, we aim to promote the accessibility and inclusivity of sports, inspiring and enabling more people to experience the life-enriching lessons it provides.

Official Statement, The AXXS Foundation

We are proud to join hands with The AXXS Foundation in their inspiring work. Through our partnership, we aim to leverage the Matchday Pro platform to increase awareness of this important cause, share inspiring stories, and encourage our users to get involved in various initiatives. We hope to contribute to a world where the values learned from sports are accessible to everyone.
